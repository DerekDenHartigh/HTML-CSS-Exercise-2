Me Piratey Life...

Me Ship

Trysail Barbary Coast nipper squiffy line rutters warp keelhaul brig yo-ho-ho. Smartly draught black jack stern gaff driver fire ship hogshead snow six pounders. Yard lad grog blossom parrel capstan keel squiffy hulk list lee. Prow pillage killick Barbary Coast walk the plank black spot grog piracy nipper swab. Sutler cutlass hardtack maroon prow gangway Privateer topmast interloper scurvy.
